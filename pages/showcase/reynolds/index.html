Your Favorite Name Brands

At Reynolds Consignment, we strive to make sure that we carry a wide selection of name brands that you as a consumer have came to love over the years.

From clothing brands and makeup to household, jewelry and electronics... our goal is to make sure that no matter what you're looking for... we will carry it at less than retail pricing.

Never Pay Full Price!

Why would you want to pay full price for something, when you can pay less? At Reynolds Consignment, we work with nearly 100 local consignors to ensure that we can carry what you want at the prices you want.

We have new sales weekly and ALWAYS have something new each and every time you visit our humble abode.

How does it work?

We are pretty easy to get along with. We offer a simple 50/50 pricing structure. You basically just bring your items in and sign a consignors agreement. We handle the rest, including pricing, advertisement, social media, storage, stocking, credit/debit card processing fees, taxes, etc.

About us.

(Original image not used in DEMO, permission needed to use likenesses)

From small town, Munfordville... to wherever you're at! Reynolds Consignment is one of the fastest growing consignment stores in Kentucky. We offer a large selection of clothing ranging in size newborn all the way to 4x. We also sell furniture, household, jewelry, electronics, makeup, and so much more!

Christian & Savannah

Reynolds Consignment is a family owned and operated business. Christian and Savannah Reynolds started Reynolds Consignment in March of 2020. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic and state regulations... they were not able to open until May 20th, 2020. Christian is a critical care paramedic, a certified fire fighter and has years of website and business experience. Savannah offers years customer service, business management and of course... the woman's touch!
